Company History

HyperTT's Video Games was founded in 2012 and specializes in selling, buying, and restoring video games and video game consoles. We specialize in any consoles from the first generation of video games to the most current generation. We offer many services including video game rental, video console repairing and restoring, and video game trading. We also have our own arcade in our store, including retro games such as Donkey Kong, Mario Brothers, and Ms. Pacman along with more current games such as Dance Dance Revolution, Mortal Kombat, and Halo. Here the fun never ends.
